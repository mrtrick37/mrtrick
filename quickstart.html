Quick Start Guide

Get gaming on Bazzite in three simple steps. No technical expertise required.

1

Download Bazzite

Head to bazzite.gg and download the ISO for your hardware. Bazzite offers editions for desktops, laptops, and gaming handhelds like the Steam Deck, ROG Ally, and Legion Go.

  • Choose between KDE Plasma or GNOME desktop
  • Nvidia drivers included out-of-the-box
  • Optimized for AMD, Intel, and Nvidia GPUs
Download Bazzite
2

Create Installation Media & Install

Flash the ISO to a USB drive using Ventoy, Balena Etcher, or Rufus. Boot from the USB and follow the simple installation wizard.

  • Guided installer walks you through each step
  • Dual-boot support for keeping Windows
  • LUKS encryption available for security
  • Installation takes 10-20 minutes
3

Start Gaming Immediately

That's it! Steam comes pre-installed with Proton ready to go. Just log in, download your games, and play. Bazzite includes all the compatibility layers, drivers, and optimizations you need.

  • Steam, Lutris, and Heroic pre-installed
  • Proton compatibility layer built-in
  • HDR and VRR support enabled
  • Performance mode for maximum FPS
  • Community game configs included
